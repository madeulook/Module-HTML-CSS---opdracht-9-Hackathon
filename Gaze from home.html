Gaze from home

„"I know, somehow, that only when it is dark enough, can you see the stars.“ - Martin Luther King

Stargazing does not have to be complicated.

It really can be as simple as looking up and noting the things you can see. If you look regularly enough you will start to notice patterns in the stars, planets moving and the moon changing its shape. That's stargazing.
